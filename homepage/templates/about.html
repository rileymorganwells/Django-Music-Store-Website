About us

FOMO Mission

Our mission is to provide professional, affordable musical instruments for students and inspire in them a love and passion for music.

History of FOMO

FOMO was started in 1995 when CEO and founder Riley Wells was trying to find a local instrument shop to buy a trumpet for his son who was starting 5th grade band. Riley couldn't find any professional, affordable instrument shops nearby, and decided to take things into his own hands and start one. FOMO has since grown to 10 employees and we provide dozens of great, affordable instruments year after year.

Accessibility

We have taken great lengths to ensure that this website is completely accessible. We use hierarchical headings, alt text for images, clear links and text, and the ability to access any part of the website from the keyboard.
